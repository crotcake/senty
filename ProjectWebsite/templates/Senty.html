SENTY
What It Is

Senty is a tool which searches articles and Twitter, and using sentiment analysis, determines the positivity of current public perception of a given company. This is a simple online tool intended to give users quick overviews of a company's perception with which they can advise their investment decisions.

How It Works

We draw data from Twitter and the news media. After filtering out posts not by real humans, we determine the general sentiment, compile it with the sentiment from all the other posts, and report the results back to you.
